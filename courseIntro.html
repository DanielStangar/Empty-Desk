STUDY INSTRUCTIONS


Each lesson consist of three parts: principle, exercise and evaluation. Those are displayed by clicking on the corresponding buttons. The principle explains you the background knowledge so that you understand why you do the exercise. The exercise itself is easy and should not take longer than ten minutes, but is the most important part of the lesson, so please, don't skip it. Doing the exercise is also the implementation of EMPTY DESK administrative system into your office. The evaluation serves you to see for yourself whether you have achieved something useful and helps you remember the studied information better.
