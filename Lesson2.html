LESSON 2


Actual reason for exhaustion from administrative or intellectual work is quite simple. You have failed to fulfill one of your goals or several of them. Not big goals or projects. Failing to fullfil small everyday task can make you so tired that you tend to drowse in your chair. The computer got mysteriously stuck and you could not answer an email. You wanted to called your supplier, but the line was busy or you weren't able to find one silly document. Such things or just one of them can make you real tired. Do you want to use this piece of knowledge to your advantage?





EXERCISE 2


Do you remember some day when you felt drained or exhausted from your office work? What are the goals you tried to achieve and could not? Write down whatever you recall, it can be one or several. As you do this exercise you may feel sudden revitalisation, lightness or return of energy.
