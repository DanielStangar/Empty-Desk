LESSON 1


Anxiety is created by doubts. If you feel unable to decide about something, it will make you think about the matter without end. Because we often cannot push it out of our mind, it can become quite uncomfortable. Indecision or doubts happen when you have two possible decisions, two possible directions and both of them seemingly of equal weigh or importance. You cannot make a choice, say the clear yes or no, or conclude which action is the best one to take. Those situations are cause of your worry and apprehenshion. Do you want to use this piece of knowledge to your advantage?





EXERCISE 1


Your task now is simple and shouldn't take more than 10 mintes. Look at your doubts or indecisions you are experiencincg right now in present days and write them downs as a list of items. Then try to establish and write down between which two or more ideas you are trying to decide. Be as sincere as possible, you don't need to tell anybody. Then go through the list again and for each doubt try to make a conclusive decision. If you don't know which could be the correct choice, go for the one that will cause less damage or less troubles.



YOUR EVALUATION


If you did the exercise, you should experience improvement in your mood. Removing doubts or indecisions often makes people feel subjectively stronger, less apprehansive, more confident, lighter, and so on. Now please use the space below, write down what you did and how your inner feelings or attitudes changed or stayed unchaged. Mere feeling of satisfaction is also valid achievement.
